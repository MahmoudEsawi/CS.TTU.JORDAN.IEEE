About Us

We are a community of passionate students dedicated to advancing computing technology and fostering professional development.

Who We Are

We are the IEEE Computer Society Chapter at Tafila Technical University. Our chapter brings together students who are interested in computers, programming, and technology, and provides them with a place to learn, share ideas, and grow.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support students in developing their skills, exploring new technologies, and preparing for future careers. We work to create opportunities that encourage both personal and professional growth.

What We Do

we provide our volunteers with hands-on exposure in their respective fields, helping them build a strong network to connect, collaborate, and grow together. We focus on empowering our community by enhancing organization and usability for our volunteers, while fostering the development and improvement of both their technical and soft skills through engaging workshops, specialized talks, competitions, and events.
